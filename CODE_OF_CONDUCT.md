# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and professional environment for everyone participating in this project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on code, design, and evidence.
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.
- Help keep discussions actionable.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Threatening, abusive, or hateful language.
- Doxxing or sharing private information without consent.
- Repeated disruption of technical discussion.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact:

- `conduct@datastrat.co`

Reports are handled confidentially and reviewed promptly.
